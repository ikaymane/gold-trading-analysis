## Overview

I've been actively trading **gold (XAUUSD)** and other financial instruments since 2022. This repository documents my approach to the markets — including analysis frameworks, trade setups, and ongoing research into price action and market structure.

Trading is one of the disciplines where you either develop real edge or you lose. After 3+ years of live market experience, this is what I've built.

---

## Approach

**Market Structure First**
I trade based on higher timeframe structure (HTF bias) before dropping to lower timeframes for entry. Identifying Break of Structure (BOS) and Change of Character (CHoCH) are the foundation.

**Key Concepts I Trade**
- Supply & Demand zones
- Order blocks and fair value gaps (FVG)
- Liquidity sweeps and stop hunts
- Session timing (London / New York opens)
- Risk-to-reward minimum 1:2, typically 1:3+

**Asset Focus**
- Primary: XAUUSD (Gold)
- Secondary: Forex majors, crypto spot
